2022 Shanghai Lockdown Diary

Welcome to the 2022 Shanghai Lockdown!

This is a game about the city-wide lock down happened in Shanghai in 2022 Spring. Although there were partial lockdowns happening in March, a city-wide lockdown was never expected by most residents in Shanghai. It came abruptly, announcing at first that there would be a four day lockdown, but in the end the lockdown was still not ended in the last day of April. Residents in Shanghai were forced to deal with all kinds of dilemmas, including food shortage, PCR tests taking place at anytime of the day, mental stress, inability to get medical attention, and a lot others.

By clicking the date below on the calendar, you are going to randomly experience one of the three games imitating typical lockdown events. If you complete the game successfully, return to the calendar and click on the next date. Have fun playing the games and enjoy this lockdown!
