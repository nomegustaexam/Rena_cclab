2022 Shanghai Lockdown Diary

Welcome to game 1: Grab your food!

People in Shanghai are facing food shortage.

In this game, you have 30 secondes to collect 30 food in total to survive the day.

Click on the food icons before they disappear. You will need to collect 30 food in total. If you can't collect enough food in time, the task fails. Please refresh the page and try it again. If you pass the game, return to the calendar by clicking the button below the canvas and continue to the game of the next date.

If you are ready, click the canvas and press key "a" to start your challenge!
