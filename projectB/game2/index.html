2022 Shanghai Lockdown Diary

Welcome to game 2: Do the PCR test!

People in Shanghai are required to do mandatory PCR tests.

In this game, you will need to line up and do the PCR test. Move the figures when the arrow indicates it is your turn to move. Do it quickly before the arrow points to the next person. The purple figures are ones you are in control of. Move them by clicking the red button the top left of the canvas.

If you move all five figures in time, you will complete the PCR test. Return to the calendar by clicking the button below the canvas and continue to the game of the next date. If figures are not moved in time, they will turn red and turn positive, and the task fails. Please refresh the page and try it again.

If you are ready, click the canvas and press key "a" to start your challenge!
