2022 Shanghai Lockdown Diary

Welcome to game 3: Complete the Homework!

Students in Shanghai are struggling to complete their homework in mental stress.

In this game, you need to complete 35 homework. Complete them by moving your character to the homework icons. Remember, you emotional level should be kept above 0 to stay in a healthy mental state to complete your homework. Avoid the angry face icons and move the happy face icons if your emotional level is low.

If your emotional level is below 0, the task fails. Please refresh the page and try it again. If you complete the 35 homework while maintaining a healthy mental state, return to the calendar by clicking the button below the canvas and continue to the game of the next date.

If you are ready, click the canvas to start your challenge!
